About Orbicular
Orbicular
Orbicular is an ERC20 token whose supply changes cyclically according to a sine wave. A week of rising supply is followed by a week of falling supply, and vice versa. Read the whitepaper.
ORBIv2
OrbicularV2 is an ERC20 token with a sinusoidally changing supply, done via rebases. It is a direct upgrade of Orbicular, allowing public rebases instead of dev-only as well as some bugfixes.
Orbet
Orbet lets anyone create a bet that any wallet will fall below any threshold number of any tokens, by any time. Anyone can participate on either side of any bet. Read the Medium article.
Azizos & Arsu (Staking)
Azizos and Arsu are two ERC20 tokens whose supplies rise and fall sinusoidally and inverse to one another. When the supply of Azizos is at its highest, the supply of Arsu is at its lowest, and vice versa. Azizos, Arsu, Azizos:ORBI liquidity, and Arsu:ORBI liquidity can all be staked for rewards. Read the whitepaper.
